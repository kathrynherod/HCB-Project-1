HOW IT WORKS

Launching a new product? Opening a new storefront? Or do you just want to engage with people who love your brand? With FanFav, we make that easy.

Create a Contest

Create a contest with a prize of your choice. Fans then submit photos based on your parameters. Maybe you'd like photos of people wearing your brand, or people taking selfies in front of your store - just post what you'd like and get awesome photos!

After Contest Creation

Interact with your fans! Vote and rate your favorite photo submissions, comment on photos, share photos, and show appreciation to your fans! After the contest ends, you pick the winner and deliver the prize! It's that easy!
